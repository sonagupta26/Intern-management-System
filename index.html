Welcome to our Intern information center

INTERN Information Systems are the primary systems for operating colleges. The Intern Information System is a student-level data collection system that allows the Department to collect and analyze more accurate and comprehensive information. Intern information systems provide capabilities for entering student records, tracking student attendance, and managing many other student-related data needs in a college or university.

Intern Information System Supports:

 

• Handling inquiries from prospective students.

• Handling the Student details.

• Maintaining the Student Marks Details.

• Handling Student Attendance Records.

• Maintaining records of absences and attendance.

• Maintaining records of Internal marks.
